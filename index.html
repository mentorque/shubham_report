This framework uses Mentorque's built-in tools — Resume Tailoring, Agentic Outreach, and Job Tracker — to systematise every application and remove the manual friction that causes most candidates to apply inconsistently.
Phase 1 — Before You Apply (Per Application)
1
Job Description Keyword Audit
Copy the full JD. Highlight every skill, tool, methodology, and qualification. Count how many appear in your current CV. Scoring rule: under 50% match → skip this role; 50–65% → tailor heavily; 65–80% → light tailoring; 80%+ → apply with summary swap only. This filter ensures you only spend effort on winnable applications.
2
One-Click Resume Tailoring
Paste the JD into Mentorque's Resume Tailoring feature. It rewrites your bullet points and skills section using the exact keywords from the JD, mirrors the job title in your summary, and injects missing terms — all in a single click. Run the output through Jobscan.co to verify ATS score above 70% before submitting. Never submit the same CV to two different roles.
⚡ Mentorque Resume Tailoring
3
ATS Format Check
Save the tailored CV as a clean single-column PDF. No tables, no text boxes, no column layouts — Irish ATS systems (Workday, Taleo, Greenhouse) parse left-to-right and silently fail on multi-column formats. Use only standard section headers. If applying to MSD, AbbVie, Pfizer, or AstraZeneca specifically, verify the output on Jobscan against their live JD before submitting.
Phase 2 — Apply & Log
4
Submit the Application
Apply through the company career portal (not Indeed Easy Apply for senior target companies — direct portal applications get full ATS scoring). For MSD, AbbVie, Kerry Group, and AstraZeneca: apply on their own career sites. For others: Indeed and IrishJobs are fine. Attach a short, role-specific cover letter (3 paragraphs: why this role, what you bring, one specific outcome from your CV).
5
Log in Job Tracker Immediately
Before moving to the next application, add the entry to Mentorque's Job Tracker: Company name, role title, date applied, CV version (name the file with the date and company), tailored Y/N, platform used, job posting URL. This takes 60 seconds and prevents the tracking collapse that causes most candidates to lose visibility of their pipeline within 2 weeks.
📊 Mentorque Job Tracker
Phase 3 — Outreach (Same Day as Application)
6
Agentic LinkedIn Outreach to Hiring Managers
Within the same day you apply, use Mentorque's Agentic Outreach to automatically send a targeted LinkedIn connection request to the Hiring Manager for that role — search by title (e.g., "Operations Manager at Kerry Group" or "SC Lead at MSD Dublin"). The connection note should reference the specific role: "Hi [Name], I've just applied for the [Role Title] position. My background in [SAP MM / production planning / Lean CI] maps closely to what you're looking for — happy to connect." This bypasses ATS consideration for shortlisting and converts at 3–5x the rate of application-only.
🤖 Mentorque Agentic Outreach — Hiring Managers
7
Agentic Outreach for Referrals — Peer-Level Professionals
Separately from hiring manager outreach, use Agentic Outreach to target people currently working in the role or team you're applying for — e.g., "Operations Analyst at MSD Dublin" or "Production Planner at Kerry Group." The message should be softer: "Hi [Name], I'm a recent MSc graduate in International Management exploring SC/ops roles in Dublin. I noticed you work in [role] at [company] — would love to connect and hear about your experience there." Peer connections lead to referrals and internal recommendations, which at Dublin pharma companies can put you in front of a recruiter directly.
🤖 Mentorque Agentic Outreach — Peer Referrals
Phase 4 — Weekly Review Loop
8
Weekly Pipeline Audit (Every Friday, 20 min)
Open Mentorque Job Tracker. Review all open applications. Apply the pivot rule: if any batch of 5+ applications returns 0 responses within 10 business days, the current positioning angle is not working. Do not send more applications — pause and diagnose first. Check: is the title right for that role category? Is the summary mirror-matching the JD? Is the ATS score above 70%? Fix the root cause before restarting. One well-positioned application beats 10 untailored ones every time.
📊 Mentorque Job Tracker
9
Follow-Up on Live Applications
For applications where you've connected with the hiring manager on LinkedIn and haven't heard back after 7 business days, send a brief follow-up message (not a new connection request — reply to the existing thread): "Hi [Name], just following up on my application for [role]. Happy to send any additional information. Looking forward to the possibility of connecting." This keeps you visible without being aggressive. Irish hiring managers respond well to polite follow-through — it signals genuine interest and professionalism.
